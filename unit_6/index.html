ABOUT

"We live in the gaps between stories."

Margaret Atwood, The Handmaid's Tale


I'm Sarah, a Chicago native now based in the DC area. I love writing, words, stories, and books. Libraries full of them. Welcome to my little nook, centered around telling stories. My own, via the blog here, but also other kinds of stories through photography and design.


Here, I've aimed to mix business and pleasure, combining freelance design work with personal reflections. Take a look at my designs by viewing my portfolio, or read my posts on life, travel, what I'm reading, and more by visiting the blog. And, of course, I hope you'll contact me if you like what you see. I'd love to help you share your story, too.


Thanks for stopping by.


xx Sarah
